Early Life

Background

My name is Bruce Wayne, a few people know me as Batman. I was born and raised in a place called Gotham City, sometimes known as New York. When I was a child, I saw my parents get robbed and killed infront of me, that was extremly traumatizing. Since I hade no other relatives, I hade 2 choices. One of them was going to an orphanage, or being taken care of by the family butler Alfred. The choice was simple, I chose Alfred. I also made a promise to avenge my parents death, and protect my city, Gotham City. Growing up I had to leave Gotham to travel the world, mature and learn how to fight just so I could return and defend my city.
